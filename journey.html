Our Journey

From idea to reality - the story of MeetYu

September 2023

First MVP Tests

We built and tested our very first MVP, gathering real feedback and validating the core idea behind MeetYu.

May 2023

Major Backing

Secured support from tech giants AWS and Microsoft, providing the resources and credibility to accelerate our journey.

July 2023

Global Team

Growing to up to 12 volunteer team members at the time, bringing diverse perspectives and energy to MeetYu.

November 2023

KTH Connection

Established a key connection with KTH, opening doors to innovation and collaboration in the Swedish tech ecosystem.

April 2024

KTH Innovation Launch Program

Selected for the prestigious KTH Innovation Launch Program, gaining access to world-class mentorship and resources.

May 2024

Beta Launch

Launched our public beta, inviting early users to experience MeetYu and help shape its future.

August 2024

AWS GenAI Program

Selected for the AWS GenAI Program to finance the build of our GenAI proof of concept, pushing the boundaries of AI-powered journaling.

Beta Launch with Knowit

Partnered with Swedish tech consultancy Knowit to release a cutting-edge, cloud-native GenAI platform with a beautiful UI/UX, giving users the experience they deserve.
