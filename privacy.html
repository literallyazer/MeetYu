6. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccuracies.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your data or account.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is processed.
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to data processing for specific purposes.

To exercise these rights you can use the features in the app, or contact us at contact@meetyu.app for questions. We will respond within 30 days.
